## 1. Introduction

### Project Overview

- website for 3D printing service
- users can either buy ready-made 3D printed products or download models for self-printing

### Objectives + What problem do we address with the project?

- provide a platform for both non-technical users and experienced users with 3D printers
- offer an intuitive shopping experience with a focus on usability and accessibility
